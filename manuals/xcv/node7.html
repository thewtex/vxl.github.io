Display

Display operations are actions that affect only the way in which VXL images and geometry are presented to the user. The following operations are under the Display menu. Some take several seconds to run.

1.
Image Magnifying Glass
Enable a magnifying glass by applying: Display .
A magnifying glass appears. Move mouse around. The area over the mouse indicator is enlarged. To disable the magnifying glass select again : Display .
