Developers

This is an incomplete list of VXL Developers, their affiliations, interests in VXL, and role in the VXL story.

This information is mainly for the benefit of other developers. If you have a question about VXL please post it to VXL-users.

Brown University

  • Joe Mundy

    Progenitor of VXL. Author of contrib/brl.

GE Global Research

  • Fred Wheeler

    Using VXL for image registration and multiple-view image enhancement. Lead maintainer of vul, vpl, and vbl.

  • Amitha Perera

    Using VXL for active contours and statistical estimation. Author of contrib/rpl. Production of testing dashboard. Lead maintainer of vnl and vgui.

University of Oxford, Robots Research Group

  • Andrew Fitzgibbon

    Progenitor of VXL. Author of vnl and vil1.

  • Fred Schaffalitzky

    Author and lead maintainer of vcl.

Leuven University

  • Peter Vanroose

    Using VXL for image object modelling and recognition. Progenitor of VXL. Lead maintainer of vgl. Production of automatic documentation, thrashing of style bandits.

University of Manchester, Imaging Science and Biomedical Engineering

  • Tim Cootes

    Using VXL for image object modelling and recognition. Author of vsl, vil and contrib/mul. Production of automatic documentation and search engine.

  • Ian Scott

    Using VXL for image/object detection. Author of contrib/mul. Author and lead maintainer of vsl and vil. Production of documentation search engine.

University of Otago, Graphics and Vision Laboratory

  • Brendan McCane

    Using VXL for real-time vision including face, eye and hand tracking, and for some medical imaging applications. Author of contrib/oul.

Kitware, Inc., Rensselaer CS Vision Group

  • Brad King

    Author of thread-safety in v3p/netlib and core/vnl. Maintainer of changes to VXL contributed through ITK. Maintainer of CMake, the build system generator for VXL.

Rensselaer CS Vision Group

  • Charles V. Stewart

    Author of robust estimation library (contrib/rpl/rrel) and generalized registration library (contrib/rpl/rgrl).

  • Michal Sofka

    Current maintainer of contrib/rpl/rgrl.

  • Gehua Yang

    Current maintainer of contrib/rpl/rgrl.
